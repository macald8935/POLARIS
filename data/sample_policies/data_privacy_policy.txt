DATA PRIVACY AND SECURITY POLICY

1. Purpose
This policy defines the organization’s approach to protecting personal and
sensitive data.

2. Scope
This policy applies to all personal data processed by the organization,
including employee and customer data.

3. Data Collection
Personal data shall be collected only for legitimate business purposes.
Only necessary data should be collected.

4. Data Usage
Collected data shall be used in accordance with business requirements.
Unauthorized use of data is prohibited.

5. Data Storage
Personal data shall be stored securely to prevent unauthorized access.

6. Data Sharing
Data may be shared with third parties when required for business operations.

7. Data Retention
Personal data should not be retained longer than necessary.

8. Incident Handling
Data breaches should be reported to management for further action.

9. Policy Review
This policy may be reviewed and updated periodically.
