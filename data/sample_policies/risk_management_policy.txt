RISK MANAGEMENT POLICY

1. Purpose
This policy establishes a framework for identifying and managing risks.

2. Scope
Applies to all organizational operations and information assets.

3. Risk Identification
Risks may be identified through assessments or operational experience.

4. Risk Treatment
Risks should be addressed based on management decisions.

5. Monitoring
Risk conditions may be monitored periodically.

6. Review
This policy may be updated as needed.
