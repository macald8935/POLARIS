INFORMATION SECURITY MANAGEMENT SYSTEM (ISMS) POLICY

1. Purpose
The purpose of this policy is to establish guidelines for protecting the
confidentiality, integrity, and availability of organizational information assets.

2. Scope
This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third parties who access
organizational information systems.

3. Asset Management
All IT assets used by the organization shall be recorded and maintained.
Asset information will be reviewed periodically.

4. Access Control
Access to systems is granted based on business requirements.
User access is approved by the reporting manager.
Access rights are assigned according to job roles.

5. Data Protection
Sensitive data shall be protected against unauthorized access.
Encryption mechanisms may be used where appropriate.

6. Patch Management
Systems shall be updated regularly to address security vulnerabilities.
Critical patches should be applied as soon as possible.

7. Monitoring
Security events may be logged and reviewed for suspicious activities.

8. Incident Management
Security incidents should be reported to the IT team.
Appropriate actions will be taken to mitigate the impact of incidents.

9. Policy Review
This policy shall be reviewed periodically and updated as necessary.
