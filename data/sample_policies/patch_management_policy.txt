PATCH MANAGEMENT POLICY

1. Purpose
This policy defines the organization’s approach to managing system patches.

2. Scope
Applies to all operating systems, applications, and network devices.

3. Patch Identification
Patches may be identified through vendor notifications or internal reviews.

4. Patch Deployment
Patches should be applied when feasible to maintain system security.

5. Exceptions
Patch exceptions may be allowed based on operational requirements.

6. Review
This policy may be reviewed periodically.
